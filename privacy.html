Privacy Policy

How FlashQuest handles your data

Effective Date: March 29, 2026

FlashQuest ("FlashQuest," "we," "our," or "us") is a flashcard and study app that helps users create, review, and practice flashcards, including with AI-powered features.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information, depending on how you use FlashQuest:

Information you provide

  • Text you submit to generate flashcards
  • Images or photos you upload for AI-powered flashcard generation
  • Flashcard content, deck names, and study content you create in the app
  • Messages you send for support

Usage and device information

  • App usage events and feature interactions
  • Device and app diagnostics
  • Basic analytics data, if enabled

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and improve FlashQuest features
  • Generate flashcards and study content
  • Support multiplayer and gameplay features
  • Diagnose bugs, improve reliability, and monitor app performance
  • Respond to support and privacy requests

AI Features

If you use FlashQuest's AI-powered features, text, notes, images, and related study content may be processed by third-party AI service providers in order to generate flashcards or related outputs.

Analytics

FlashQuest may collect optional analytics to help us improve the app. You can disable optional analytics in the app at any time.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate FlashQuest, such as hosting, analytics, infrastructure, and AI-processing providers, strictly for app functionality, support, and improvement.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, maintain operations, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, and improve our services.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Disable optional analytics in the app
  • Contact us with privacy-related questions
  • Contact us for support requests

Children's Privacy

FlashQuest is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page and revise the Effective Date above.
