Privacy Policy

How FlashQuest handles your data

Effective Date: April 5, 2026

FlashQuest ("FlashQuest," "we," "our," or "us") is a flashcard and study app that helps users create, review, and practice flashcards. FlashQuest includes AI-powered features, social features, real-time multiplayer, and a community deck library.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information, depending on how you use FlashQuest:

Account information

  • Email address (for account creation and authentication)
  • Display name and username (shown to other users on leaderboards, friend lists, and published decks)
  • Authentication tokens from Apple Sign In or email-based login

Study content you create

  • Flashcard content, deck names, descriptions, and categories
  • Text, notes, images, or documents you submit for AI-powered flashcard generation
  • Decks you publish to the community library

Study progress and activity

  • Study session results, scores, streaks, and XP
  • Spaced repetition scheduling data for your cards
  • Leaderboard rankings and statistics

Social and multiplayer data

  • Friend connections and friend requests
  • Challenge invitations and results
  • Arena battle room data, scores, and player activity during matches

Device and notification data

  • Push notification tokens (to deliver friend requests, challenge invitations, and deck notifications)
  • Device type and platform information

Usage information

  • App usage events and feature interactions (optional, can be disabled)
  • Basic analytics and diagnostics data

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide and improve FlashQuest features including study modes, spaced repetition, and progression
  • Generate flashcards and study content using AI
  • Enable social features including friends, challenges, and leaderboards
  • Enable real-time multiplayer Arena battles
  • Operate the community deck library where users can publish and discover decks
  • Send push notifications for friend requests, challenges, and deck updates
  • Diagnose bugs, improve reliability, and monitor app performance
  • Moderate community content and enforce our terms of service
  • Respond to support and privacy requests

AI Features

If you use FlashQuest's AI-powered features (Text to Deck, Scan Notes, or AI-assisted study), text, notes, images, documents, and related study content may be processed by third-party AI service providers in order to generate flashcards, hints, explanations, or related outputs.

Community Decks

When you publish a deck to the community library, your username, deck name, description, category, and card content become visible to other FlashQuest users. You can unpublish your decks at any time from the Explore screen. Decks that receive multiple community reports may be automatically hidden.

Push Notifications

FlashQuest may send push notifications for friend requests, challenge invitations, and community deck updates. You can manage notification permissions in your device settings at any time.

Analytics

FlashQuest may collect optional analytics to help us improve the app. You can disable optional analytics in the app at any time through the Data and Privacy settings.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate FlashQuest, such as cloud hosting (Supabase), AI-processing providers, and infrastructure services, strictly for app functionality, support, and improvement. Your username, display name, and published deck content are visible to other FlashQuest users through social features and the community library.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, maintain operations, resolve disputes, comply with legal obligations, and improve our services. You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Disable optional analytics in the app
  • Manage push notification permissions in your device settings
  • Unpublish decks you have shared to the community
  • Remove friends and decline friend requests
  • Request deletion of your account by contacting privacy@flashquest.net
  • Contact us with privacy-related questions

Children's Privacy

FlashQuest is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page and revise the Effective Date above.
