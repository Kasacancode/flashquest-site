Features

Four ways to study.
All of them actually work.

Flashcards don't have to be boring. FlashQuest gives you spaced repetition, timed quizzes, AI practice, and head-to-head battles.

Arena Mode

Battle friends in real time

The only flashcard app with live multiplayer. Create a room, share a QR code, and go head-to-head. Fastest correct answer wins each round.

  • QR code or room code to join instantly
  • Pick any deck, yours or community
  • Live scoring with round-by-round results
  • Challenge friends or study with classmates
Quest Mode

Timed quizzes that keep you sharp

Multiple-choice rounds with a timer, streak multipliers, and XP rewards. Choose Learn mode for practice or Test mode when you want real pressure.

  • Configurable rounds, timer, and hints
  • Streak multipliers reward consistency
  • AI assistant gives round-by-round coaching
  • Earn XP toward your next level
Study Mode

Spaced repetition that adapts to you

Cards you struggle with show up more often. Cards you've mastered fade away. Rate each card as Easy, Okay, Hard, or Forgot, and the algorithm handles the rest.

  • Mastery tracking per card: new, learning, reviewing, mastered
  • Due card count tells you what to study today
  • Hints and explanations on every card
  • Zero configuration needed
AI + Community

AI makes your cards. Students share theirs.

Type a topic, paste your notes, or scan a photo. AI generates a complete deck in seconds. Or browse 50+ community decks across science, languages, business, history, and more.

  • AI deck creation from text, notes, or photos
  • 50+ curated community decks in 10 categories
  • Import from Quizlet, Anki, CSV, and more
  • Publish your own decks for others to use
How It Works

From notes to A's in three steps.

No setup guides. No learning curve. Open the app and start studying within 60 seconds.

1

Build your deck

Scan notes, paste text, import from Quizlet or Anki, or let AI generate cards on any topic. Your deck is ready in under a minute.

2

Study your way

Flip through cards with spaced repetition, race the clock in Quest, practice against AI, or challenge a friend to an Arena battle.

3

Actually remember it

The app tracks what you know and what's fading. Smart reminders bring you back at the right time. Streaks keep you accountable.
